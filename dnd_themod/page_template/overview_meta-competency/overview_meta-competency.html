Type "meta-competency based"

The structure / model type "meta-competence based" approach emphasizes the logic of a to constructivist knowledge construction. It moves from the perspective of metacognition and development of knowledge and skills in a way that promotes systematic use by the individual and the group of survey instruments direct (attitudes, methods, techniques) that open to the possibility of conceptualization, generalization, the transferability of knowledge products and methods used to produce them.
